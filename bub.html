Lore

Lore of Lil Bub: Lil Bub, the mystical kitten with eyes as wide as the full moon, is an unlikely but fierce ally to Milo in the battle against darkness. Born under a rare celestial event, she was destined to be a companion to the hero of Eldermoor. Unlike other cats, Lil Bub was blessed with a unique charm: the ability to harness the power of the stars themselves. Her small size and quirky demeanor often belied her strength, but those who underestimated her quickly learned the error of their ways. Raised in the secluded corners of the enchanted forest, Lil Bub was trained in the ancient ways of the Feline Astrals, a secret order of magical cats who could channel the energy of the heavens. Her powers, though unrefined at first, grew stronger with Milo’s guidance, and soon she became an invaluable part of the team, using her star-infused magic to aid in their adventures.

  • Celestial Barrier – By weaving threads of starlight, Lil Bub can create shimmering protective barriers around her allies. These ethereal shields absorb incoming damage, providing defense against physical and magical attacks. The barrier glows with the energy of the stars, shimmering brightly in the darkest of places.
  • Starflare Burst – Lil Bub can focus her celestial power into concentrated bursts of blinding light, stunning and disorienting enemies. This explosive burst blinds foes temporarily, leaving them vulnerable to further attacks while also disrupting dark magic, making it difficult for shadow-based enemies to focus or aim.
  • Astral Traverse – Lil Bub can temporarily shift into the astral plane, becoming a fleeting wisp of starlight. This ability allows her to travel between the stars, evading danger, sneaking past enemies, or quickly navigating across realms. It makes her an unpredictable and elusive force, able to outmaneuver even the most powerful foes
