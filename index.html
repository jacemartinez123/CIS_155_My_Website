Lore

Milo was no ordinary dog. Born in the tranquil village of Eldermoor, a place hidden deep in the enchanted forest, he was a small terrier with an unassuming stature, but a heart brimming with adventure. His story began when an ancient, magical oak tree in the village’s heart whispered his name in a vision to the village elder, predicting that a small dog would one day save their land from an impending darkness. At the time, no one took the prophecy seriously—after all, how could a tiny dog change the fate of a whole kingdom? But Milo was destined for greatness, though he didn’t yet know it. One fateful evening, the skies above Eldermoor darkened, and a creeping mist rolled over the land, signaling the arrival of an ancient curse. The village was soon overrun by shadowy beasts and monstrous creatures, sent forth by the malevolent sorceress Morgana, who sought to claim the forest’s magical heart for herself. With his village under threat, Milo’s true potential awakened. He discovered that his lineage traced back to the legendary Canis Luminis, a long-forgotten order of magical guardians who once protected the forest. His small size belied his newfound abilities: Milo could summon light to banish darkness, command the winds to carry him across great distances, and even communicate with the forest creatures who rallied to his side. Milo led a daring charge against Morgana’s forces, rallying an army of woodland creatures, from swift foxes to wise old owls, all eager to defend their home. In an epic battle under the glow of a full moon, Milo confronted Morgana herself. With a flash of blinding light and a bark that echoed through the trees, he shattered her dark spell, breaking the curse and restoring peace to the land. Though the villagers now hailed him as their hero, Milo remained humble, always content to chase after butterflies in the meadows and curl up beside the village hearth, knowing that his small paws had helped protect a great and magical world.

Milo's dislikes

  • Darkness – As a guardian of light, Milo has an intense dislike for darkness and anything that seeks to spread it, especially the shadows that once plagued his village.
  • Bullies – Milo doesn’t tolerate any form of cruelty, especially toward weaker creatures. He’s quick to stand up to anyone who harms others.
  • Loud, Chaotic Noises – Milo prefers quiet moments and gets anxious in overly noisy or chaotic situations, particularly when they disrupt the peace of Eldermoor.
  • Being Underestimated – Despite his small size, Milo doesn’t like when others doubt his abilities. He knows that true strength comes from within, not from outward appearances.

Milo's likes

  • Adventures in the Forest – Milo loves exploring the enchanted woods around Eldermoor, where he feels the most connected to his magical heritage.
  • Helping Others – Whether it’s guiding lost travelers or protecting his village, Milo enjoys using his abilities to help those in need.
  • Chasing Light – Milo has a fascination with light, especially when he can summon it to chase away darkness or illuminate the night.
  • Friendship with Woodland Creatures – Milo enjoys spending time with his woodland allies, from swift foxes to wise owls, feeling at home among them.
  • Cuddling by the Hearth – After a long day of protecting the village, Milo loves curling up by the fire, resting and recharging with the villagers.
